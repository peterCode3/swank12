Our Services

Phone Consultation

Our initial phone consultations are always free. We will discuss your goals and make a plan for moving forward.

On-Site Evaluation

We come on site and see your current operations, interview staff and get feedback from your entire team and make recommendations as needed.

Online Evaluation

We connect via online meetings to review current operations and interview your team to determine what is working and what is not and make recommendations as needed.

Procurement Specification Development

We work hand in hand with you to develop the specifications for your RFP/RFQ that are custom to your needs. Each school district is different, so we want to make sure you are not buying more or less than what you actually need or copying an RFP from a neighbor.

Proposal Review Services

We will help you review and understand all responses to your Request for Proposal so you can make educated decisions with your selection process.

Implementation/Audit

After your purchase we will work with you to make sure everything is implemented to its full potential. We will also complete a bid audit within 12 months of implementation to make sure all specifications are being met as described in the vendor bid response.
